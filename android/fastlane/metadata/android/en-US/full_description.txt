Swipe, merge, and discover the most amazing animals from the Amazon!

Olha o Bichim! (Hey, Look at the Little Animal!) is a 2048-style puzzle game with a Brazilian twist. Merge matching tiles to evolve your animals — from the tiny Leafcutter Ant all the way to the legendary Capybara!

🐜 HOW TO PLAY
Swipe to move all animals at once. When two matching animals meet, they fuse into a rarer species. Reach 2048 to find the Legendary Capybara!

🌿 AMAZON WILDLIFE
Discover 13 real animals from Brazil's diverse biomes — from the Cerrado to the Amazon rainforest:
Leafcutter Ant • Maned Wolf • Cane Toad • Toucan • Marmoset • Sloth • Golden Lion Tamarin • Amazon River Dolphin • Jaguar • Anaconda • Legendary Capybara and more!

⚡ SPECIAL ITEMS
Use Bombs to remove tiles from the board and Undo moves to go back when things get tough!

🎁 DAILY REWARDS
Collect rewards every day and double your prizes by watching a short ad.

🏆 GLOBAL RANKING
Compete with players from Brazil and around the world on the weekly leaderboard. Reach 2048, 4096, or 8192 to enter the Legends Hall!

🎵 MUSIC & ANIMATIONS
Original soundtrack inspired by Brazilian bossa nova and jazz. Unique procedural sound effects for every animal.

✨ NO MOVE LIMIT
Play at your own pace — no timers, no pressure. Think, merge, and have fun!
